# Parallelogram Area Problem — Solution

## Part 1

Note that $AE : ED : CB : BF = 2 : 5 : 7 : 5$.

$$
\Delta AEG \sim \Delta BFG \text{ (AAA)}
$$

$$
\left(\frac{AE}{BF}\right)^2 = \frac{\text{Area of } \Delta AEG}{\text{Area of } \Delta BFG}
$$

$$
\left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^2 = \frac{48}{\text{Area of } \Delta BFG}
$$

$$
\text{Area of } \Delta BFG = 300
$$

$$
\frac{CB}{BF} = \frac{\text{Area of } \Delta CBG}{\text{Area of } \Delta FBG}
$$

$$
\frac{7}{5} = \frac{\text{Area of } \Delta CBG}{300}
$$

$$
\text{Area of } \Delta CBG = 420
$$

Note that $DEFB$ is a parallelogram, $DB \mathrel{//} EF$.

$$
\therefore \Delta CHB \sim \Delta CGF
$$

---

## Part 2

Since $CB = 7k$, $BF = 5k$, we have $CF = 12k$, and

$$
\text{Area of } \Delta CGF = 420 + 300 = 720
$$

$$
\left(\frac{CB}{CF}\right)^2 = \frac{\text{Area of } \Delta CHB}{\text{Area of } \Delta CGF}
$$

$$
\left(\frac{7}{12}\right)^2 = \frac{\text{Area of } \Delta CHB}{720}
$$

$$
\text{Area of } \Delta CHB = 245
$$

$$
\text{Area of } \Delta GBH = 420 - 245 = 175
$$

Note that $\Delta AEG \sim \Delta BFG$ (AAA).

$$
AG : GB : DC = 2 : 5 : 7
$$

$$
\Delta GBH \sim \Delta CDH \text{ (AAA)}
$$

$$
\left(\frac{GB}{CD}\right)^2 = \frac{\text{Area of } \Delta GBH}{\text{Area of } \Delta CDH}
$$

$$
\left(\frac{5}{7}\right)^2 = \frac{175}{\text{Area of } \Delta CDH}
$$

$$
\text{Area of } \Delta CDH = 343 \text{ cm}^2
$$

---

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{\text{Area of } \Delta CDH = 343 \text{ cm}^2}
$$
